World Cup 2013

2013 has been a Golden Year for Enactus DTU. Our hard work finally paid off when we were declared Winners at the National Championship in June 2013 in Mumbai. Not only did it put us on the Enactus India Hall of Fame, just two years after starting operations, it also gave us the opportunity to represent India at the 2013 Enactus World Cup in Cancun, Mexico!

No words can describe the magic, wonder and pride that we felt while competing at the World Cup. It provided us with a tremendous platform to showcase our projects in front of the entire Enactus Community comprising of Students, Teachers and Business Leaders.

We also learnt a great deal about Project Sustainability and got brilliant Project ideas by interacting with students of Enactus teams from 34 countries. Enactus DTU had a dream run at the World Cup as we were the League Winners in our first round and reached the Semi Finals.

We were declared one of the top 16 teams in the World out of the 1600 University teams functioning all around the world!
