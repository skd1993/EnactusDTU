What We Do.....

Enactus is an international non-profit organization that brings together student, academic and business leaders who are committed to using the power of entrepreneurial action to improve the quality of life and standard of living for people in need.

Enactus is operational in 36 countries, with over 62,000 students working in 1600 University teams!

Enactus Delhi Technological University (DTU)

We started operations in February 2011 and are currently working on three projects namely

  1. Project Ann (Estd. 2011)
  2. Project Chhaap (Estd. 2012)
  3. Project Unmoolan (Estd. 2012)

Today, we are a team of over 56 members, working towards the goal of a sustainable future. In a short span of two years, our team has been crowned the National Champions in 2013 and was a semi-finalist at the Enactus World Cup 2013!

We plan to continue on the positivity and momentum and achieve even greater milestones in the future through our dedication and creativity.
