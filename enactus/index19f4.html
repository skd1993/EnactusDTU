PROJECT CHHAAP

Project Ideology:

Chhaap means “imprint”. Chhaap aims at improving the socio-economic conditions of the people of the Chattarpur slum by empowering women and the elderly and providing them with a sustainable source of income. We also believe in creating a sustainable future and are urging stores to replace the existing plastic bags with Chhaap printed cloth bags.

Success Story:

Chhaap not only provides a regular income to the women and elderly of the Chattarpur slum, but has also given them a deep sense of purpose and achievement. They are now capable of making an independent living and can supplement their family’s incomes. The women can now send their children to school and the elderly no longer have to depend on their sons for medical treatment. We opened personal Bank Accounts for the women and trained them in the basics of financial planning to ensure that the control of their earnings remained with them. The success of Chhaap printed bags lay in our competitive prices, high quality printing, and the fact that we are changing lives.

How we will make it Bigger:

In today’s tech savvy world, we plan to move with the times. We aim to start our own line of bags which will be channeled through a website. Slowly, by incorporating E-commerce, all business transactions will happen online. We hope to make Chhaap a well-known brand name in the market which will be known for good quality, creative designs and the harbinger of social and environmental change.
